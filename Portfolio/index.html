Hello, I'm Dianne! I'm a Data Analyst in training based in the Bay Area, California. My primary interest is the visualization of data whether it be on Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides, Python Matplotlib, Javascript Plotly, or manipulating HTML and CSS with Bootstrap 4.

I used to be in the adminstrative industry and my focus was working in the non-profit and government field. After five years in the industry I wanted to carry over my passion for serving the community to the tech world! I enjoyed optimizing administrative processes by establishing standard procedures and creating training documentation. I was the first to introduce a standard reporting template of our Surgery Department patient data which led to standardized surgery documentation for each electronic health record on a hospital wide scale.

I'm currently pursuing a Data Analytics and Visualization certification at UC Berkeley. So far I've gained skills in Micrsoft Excel VBA, Python, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy ORM queries, Bootstrap 4 HTML and CSS, and DOM Manipulation with D3. I'm excited on my career path from growing to a data analyst, through data extraction and visualization; to Data Visualization Analyst and Web Designer by delivering complex data and products in a useful, appealing, and user-friendly way.
