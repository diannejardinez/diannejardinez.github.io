Research question: "What's the weather like as we approach the equator?"

Analyzing a series of scatter plots to showcase the following relationships to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator.

    Focusing on these general weather parameters:
  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude

    Focusing on these specific latitude parameters with the weather parameters:
  • Temperature (F) vs. Northern and Southern Hemisphere Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Northern and Southern Hemisphere Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Northern and Southern Hemisphere Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Northern and Southern Hemisphere Latitude
