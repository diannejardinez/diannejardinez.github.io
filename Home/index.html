Summary: Weather and Latitude

Purpose: Analyzing a series of scatter plots to showcase the following relationships to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. Focusing on these general weather parameters: Temperature (F) vs. Latitude, Humidity (%) vs. Latitude, Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude, and Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude.
